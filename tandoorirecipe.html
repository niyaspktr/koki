How to Prepare Tandoori

Mix lemon juice, ginger paste, two tablespoon oil/butter, curd, salt and KOKI tandoori Masala. Marinate cut chicken with above mixture. Pick all over the meat. Keep aside for the 30 minutes. Apply strained yogurt and set aside for 3 hours. Arrange the pieces in a tray or cooking grill. Burn the chicken until cooked. You can serve your tandoori with salad and lime pieces
