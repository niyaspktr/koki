How to Prepare Al Faham

Clean and wash whole chicken. cut it into pieces lengthwise. Using a fork or knife pick the chicken well so that the marination goes inside the chicken. Wash chicken in salt water. Marinate the with this paste KOKI Al Faham masala and keep in fridge for three to four hours. It's good if you can leave it over night. Grill evenly on each side until meat is tender and done.

Serving Suggestions:
Serve hot Al Faham chicken with fresh green salad, sliced onion, tomatoes and BBQ/Green Chutney.
